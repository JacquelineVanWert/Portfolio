About Me
Hello, I'm Jacqueline VanWert,
it's nice to meet you!

I am a UI/UX designer currently living in Tampa, Florida. In 2022 I graduated from a UI/UX bootcamp based from the University of Central Florida. The thing I love most about being a designer is using my skills as a digital artist to determine an aesthetically pleasing design that focuses on the general feel of the product. Using research and logic, I enjoy working with a design team to understand how we proceed with each new idea. Creating beautiful and functional designs with a strong tie to a brand identity is my passion.
